How We Track Time
Our billing records method for this consulting engagement

Team Group 5 operates as a UX consulting team at a rate of $100/hour/person. All work — including meetings, research, design, prototyping, and documentation — is tracked in a shared Google Sheet timesheet. Every meeting is logged with a calendar entry and meeting notes, and we produce a weekly per-person summary.

Fields Recorded in the Timesheet

  • Date — When the work was performed
  • Person — Team member who did the work
  • Activity Type — Meeting, research, design, development, documentation, etc.
  • Description — Brief description of the activity
  • Hours — Time spent in decimal hours
